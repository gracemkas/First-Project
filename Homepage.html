GRACE KASAHARA

I graduated from St. Olaf College in 2014 with a bachelor's degree in psychology. After graduating, I spent a year teaching basic computer skills and programming using Scratch and MIT App Inventor through AmeriCorps in a low-income community. This gave me valuable teaching skills that I was then able to apply to my next job of teaching English to middle and elementary school students in Japan. When I returned to the United States, I entered a master's degree program in clinical psychology where I developed my interest in human-computer interaction. After I graduate in May of this year, I intend to work on my skills in computer programming and design.
